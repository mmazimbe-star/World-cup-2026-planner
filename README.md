# Matchday Planner — World Cup 2026 🏆

A interactive, browser-based planning tool for the FIFA World Cup 2026, hosted across USA, Canada, and Mexico.

**[Open the planner →](https://mmazimbe-star.github.io/World-cup-2026-planner/)**

---

## What it does

Set scores for all 48 group-stage matches across 12 groups and watch everything update live:

- **Group planner** — standings update in real time with points, goal difference, and status badges (Won Group, Through, Projected, 3rd Race, Eliminated)
- **Best thirds** — automatically ranks all 12 third-place finishers by FIFA tiebreaker rules and shows the cut line for the 8 who advance
- **Knockout bracket** — fills in from your group results; tap any team to advance them round by round all the way to the final

Tiebreakers follow FIFA order: points → goal difference → goals scored → head to head.

---

## How to use

1. Open the planner link above
2. Enter scores for group matches — standings and the bracket update instantly
3. Hit **Autofill favourites** to quickly populate a projected bracket
4. Switch to **Knockout bracket** and tap teams to pick your winners
5. Share the link with friends and compare predictions

---

## About

Built as a single self-contained HTML file — no frameworks, no dependencies, no server required. Works in any modern browser, supports dark mode, and is mobile-friendly.

The final is at MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford NJ on Sun, Jul 19, 2026.
